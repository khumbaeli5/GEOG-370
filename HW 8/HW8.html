A study of incursion of managed areas

In this study I searched to answer the proposed effect of Transmission lines within managed wildlife areas in North Carolina.
Transmission lines emit radiation and low frequnecy sound, that even if invisible to humans, are very hurflut to wildlife.
In order to analyse the impact of the effects I first found a shapfile of the transmittion lines within North Carolina. However, there does not seem to exist one that is a line
As such I grabed a shapefile of all the transmission lines in the US and used an intersection to narrow it down to NC.
From there I was able to use intersection and a buffer in order to exemplify the impact of the transmission lines
